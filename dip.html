Quick Dip

Description

It is an easy addition to any party. Quick and easy--what more can you ask for in a recipe?

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar mayonnaise
  • 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix

Steps

  1. Combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, and ranch dressing mix in a large bowl. Cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.
